Liqui Molly

Redesigned the vehicle oil guide to improve the user experience.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Tools

Figma, Miro

Timeline

6 weeks

Context

The LIQUI MOLY app is an efficient solution for swiftly identifying the optimal products for your vehicle. The app also guides you to the nearest locations where you can purchase LIQUI MOLY products, offers valuable insights on lubricants, additives, care products and provides expert tips on vehicle maintenance. However, after seeing the feedback from users, I decided to improve the overall experience of this app.

My aim for this app is to contribute to this app which addresses the current challenges and feedback from users.
