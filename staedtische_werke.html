Städtische Werke

EcoSync is designed to help flatmates optimize energy consumption.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Tools

Figma, Miro

Timeline

3 weeks

Context

EcoSync is a user-friendly mobile application designed to assist students and individuals living in shared flats in optimizing energy consumption during the moving-out or relocating process.

It aims to revolutionize the way shared living spaces manage energy, fostering a collaborative and sustainable approach that benefits both the residents and the environment.

Problem

In shared living spaces, particularly among students and individuals in shared flats, there is a pervasive lack of streamlined communication and coordination regarding energy consumption and responsibilities during the moving-out process. This leads to an overall inefficient and stressful relocation experience.

The absence of a centralized platform exacerbates challenges such as unequal distribution of energy expenses, difficulties in managing individual energy contracts, and a general lack of awareness regarding energy-efficient practices.

How can we empower residents in shared living spaces to efficiently manage their energy consumption, fostering a system where individual contracts streamline the moving-out process for each resident?

Goal

The goal of this app is to empower students and residents in shared flats to seamlessly manage and reduce energy consumption.

It also aims to allow flexibility during relocation while facilitating fair expense distribution among flat residents with individual energy contracts.

Key needs

Challenges in fairly dividing energy costs among residents.

Lack of a streamlined process during the moving-in and moving-out process.

Absence in managing individual energy contracts with providers.

Difficulties for the individual to track the expenses.

Insights

  • ● Residents in shared flats prefer having individual contracts with energy providers and managing their energy payments independently.
  • ● The moving-out process can be a significant source of stress.
  • ● There is a general lack of awareness about individual energy consumption and its environmental impact.
  • ● Residents often experience changes in living arrangements based on academic or personal needs.

User research

I conducted six user research interviews, delving into the experiences and preferences of students and individuals living in shared flats. The goal was to understand their perspectives on optimizing energy consumption during the moving-out or relocating process and to explore how it could revolutionize energy management in shared living spaces.

Ideation

EcoSync envisions a user-centric experience, addressing pain points by providing a seamless and informative journey. The aim for this app is to revolutionize the way individuals in shared flats manage and reduce their energy consumption during relocations. My focus was in bringing simplicity, accessibility, and fostering a sense of community to encourage sustainable living practices.

Wireframes

I created some low-fidelity wireframes and brainstormed various ideas and features aimed at enhancing user experience and aligning with their specific goals.
